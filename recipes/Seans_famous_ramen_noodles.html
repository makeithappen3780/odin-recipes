Sean's Famous Ramen Noodles

Description

This one is also pretty simple. You can tell I was once a ordinary college student. Take your ramen level to the next level with this simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodle of your choice
  • Egg
  • Your choice of protein
  • Your choice of greens
  • Frozen fish balls
  • Kimchi
  • Splenda

Steps

  1. Boil recommmended amount of water in a pot
  2. Either steam frozen fishballs over the top of the water, or add fishballs directly into the boiling water
  3. After the fishballs are done cooking, add the noodles into the boiling water
  4. Along with the noodles, crack an egg and add it into the boiling water. Reduce heat to medium to avoid egg fluffing from the rapid boil
  5. After noodles and egg are done cooking, take pan off heat and add in green leafy vegetables
  6. Add in a sprinkle of pepper, cinnamon, and one pack of Splenda or brown sugar. I use splenda myself to add a little bit of sweetness to the broth
  7. Enjoy Sean's Famous Ramen Noodles
