Sean's Cinnammon Coffee

Description

Here's a nice simple recipe for the lazy folks who just so happen to like coffee. It's literally one extra step to your regular coffee making process (assuming you are using a standard coffee machine) to make your coffee taste that much more delicious.

Ingredients

  • Coffee Ground
  • Water
  • Cinnammon Powder

Steps

  1. Add water to coffee machine
  2. Add coffee ground to filter
  3. Add light coating of powdered cinnammon to the top of the coffee grounds
  4. Brew coffee
  5. Enjoy your nice cinnammon coffee!
